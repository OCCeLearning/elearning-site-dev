Online Course Syllabus Library

The e-Learning department is excited to announce the launch of our online syllabus library. Head on over and explore the syllabi for all of our online courses.

Many of our online courses are offered with a variety of term lengths. Our library will allow you to explore syllabi using the term dropdown menu. Here you can find syllabi based on the term length that works for your goals.

Plagiarism Policy

OCC has a strict academic dishonesty policy to maintain the integrity of the courses and degree programs. Students are expected to follow this policy for all assignments, discussions, exercises, and exams in both face-to-face and online classes.

Read the Academic Dishonesty Policy to learn more.

Please access the Responding to Academic Integrity Violations document for information on the steps you should follow if your online professor says that your paper contains plagiarism.

Attendance Policy

Tutoring is available online accessible from your Canvas course menu. You will have an initial two-hour block of time which you can always add more. Additional resources available to you are the OCC Counseling Center and Library.
Click the “SmartThinking” icon in Canvas for tutoring.

Face-to-Face tutoring is available on campus.
Visit the Tutoring Page to learn more.
Research assistance is also available at the library. Visit the Library Page to learn more.
