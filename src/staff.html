Department Mission

The e-Learning Department strives to serve our students by offering stimulating and rewarding educational experiences through the use of technology, innovation, and effective design to develop and deliver high-quality, engaging, and academically-rich distance learning courses. The services of e-Learning shall be of the highest quality and have a local, domestic, and international reach.

OCC's online learning programs are designed to give students of all ages the opportunity to earn their degree. The goal of the e-Learning department is to ensure all students have the tools and guidance necessary for success.

Dr. Eileen Garcia
Vice President of E-Learning & Learning Enterprises
