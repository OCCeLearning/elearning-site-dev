A Message from the Dean

Our online programs and courses are entirely online and asynchronous, which means students are never required to come to campus and are not required to be "online" during a specific day or time. Your courses will follow the same academic rigor as traditional face-to-face courses, utilizing the same learning outcomes and employing a variety of student-centered learning methodologies. Ocean County College utilizes Canvas, a state-of-the-art online Learning Management System platform to keep you engaged with your instructors and peers as you progress towards your degree.

Take a look around our site, get to know our team, and see if e-Learning is a great fit for you. We are ready to help you find your path to success!
